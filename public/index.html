About Me

Who I Am

Cat owning, New York native with a lifelong passion for technology. I started coding as a hobby while studying Information Technology at the University of South Carolina. What began as a hobby quickly grew into an obsession. Soon after starting I knew that persuing web dev as a carear was something I had to do.

After graduation, I enrolled in a coding boot camp at UNC Charlotte to focus my self learning and surround myself with other intelligent and passionate people. There, I formed a solid foundation and learned the most in demand technologies.

Why I do This

I code becuase I love to make things. Starting with a blank screen and ending with a finished application is one of the most satisfying feelings I can imagine. I'm at my happiest when using my brain and creativity and working with others to solve problems.

I believe technology is the most powerfull problem solving tool we have. I want to spend my carear contributing to projects that leverage technology in new and intresting ways and be apart of an Indusry that is driving us forward.

Roll PG

An online assistant to pen and paper role playing games like Dungeons and Dragons. Roll PG will handle all of the dice rolls and complex math involved in role playing games and allows the user to focus on the adventure at hand. Players can create and play as their own characters or, (thanks to the magic of web sockets) host their own shared role playing session as a dungeon master. You create the adventure. Roll PG (and your immagination) will bring it to life

Created using: Node, Express, Handlebars, Socket.io, BootStrap, Passport, Javascript, Jquery, MySql and Sequelize

Concert Buddy

Does your weird taste in music leave you dragging unwanting friends along to concerts? Thanks to Concert Buddy, your ecclectic taste in music will never isolate you again! Users can search for shows in their area and at the click of a button, let the world know they're looking for a friend to meet up with at the concert. Users attending the same show can messege each other via facebook and start a friendship that may last a lifetime.

Created using: BootStrap, Javascript, Jquery, facebook API, and firebase
