RollPg
Roll PG

An online assistant to pen and paper roll playing games like Dungeons and Dragons. Roll PG will handle all of the dice rolls and complex math involved in roll playing games and allows the user to focus on the adventure at hand. Players can create and play as their own characters or, (thanks to the magic of web sockets) host their own shared role playing session as a dungeon master. You create the adventure. Roll PG (and your immagination) will bring it to life

Created using: Node, Express, Socket.io, BootStrap, Passport, Javascript, Jquery, MySql and Sequelize

Concert Buddy
Concert Buddy

Does your weird taste in music leave you dragging unwanting friends along to concerts? Thanks to Concert Buddy, your ecclectic taste in music will never isolate you again! Users can search for shows in their area and at the click of a button, let the world know they're looking for a friend to meet up with at the concert. Users attending the same show can messege each other via facebook and start a friendship that may last a lifetime.

Created using: BootStrap, Javascript, Jquery, facebook API, and firebase
